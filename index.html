The Refined Voices

A grounding ritual for speaking

The Refined Voices is an instruction-based participatory object system that connects the act of speaking to a ritual of shaving material away and archiving it to show what was shed in order to speak. Designed to help people feel calmer and more present while speaking in group discussions.

Before speaking, begin shaving or reducing a material away. While speaking, they continue this action. The motion regulates breath, slows the body, and transforms nervous energy into focus. The material gradually diminishes. The shavings fall and are collected in a clear container, forming a visible archive of collective speech over time.

How It Works

1. Pick up your chosen material object.
2. Begin shaving, peeling, or reducing it.
3. Speak while sharpening.
4. Place the shavings in a clear container.

Participation is optional. The ritual is meant to support, not control, conversation.
